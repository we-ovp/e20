A Brief on E20

  • E20 is a fuel blend of 20% ethanol and 80% petrol, launched nationwide in India by 2025 to promote cleaner energy and reduce oil imports.
  • The ethanol is produced locally from crops like sugarcane and corn, supporting farmers and boosting the rural economy.
  • E20 fuel reduces carbon emissions by about 30% compared to E10 and improves vehicle acceleration due to higher octane levels.
  • Older vehicles not designed for E20 may face engine issues and reduced fuel efficiency, while newer models post-2023 are generally compatible.
  • India aims to fully adopt E20 fuel by 2025 as a step toward energy security, environmental goals, and rural development.
